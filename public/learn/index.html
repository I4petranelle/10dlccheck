Why 10DLC Compliance Matters

10DLC (10-Digit Long Code) is the U.S. standard for Application-to-Person (A2P) messaging. Carriers require registration and compliant messaging to reduce spam and protect consumers.

Non-compliant messages get filtered, delayed, or blocked—costing you money and trust. These guides explain the rules, common pitfalls, and practical steps to keep your messages deliverable.

Quick Compliance Tips

  • Identify your brand clearly in the first message.
  • Include opt-out instructions (STOP) in promotional messages.
  • Avoid public link shorteners (use branded or full URLs).
  • Avoid SHAFT content (Sex, Hate, Alcohol, Firearms, Tobacco).
  • Collect and document consent before sending messages.
  • Honor opt-out requests promptly.
