Pre-Send Compliance Checklist

Run this quick checklist before sending to reduce blocks, complaints and carrier fees.

By 10dlccheck Editorial • Published October 16, 2025 • Updated October 18, 2025 • ~3 min read

Use this quick checklist before sending your campaign. Tick each box to verify compliance — your progress will update automatically.

Audience & Consent

  • All contacts have **documented opt-in**.
  • Opt-in clearly states **brand + purpose**.
  • **No purchased or shared lists**.

Message Content

  • Brand name appears in the message.
  • Content is **truthful** and matches the **approved campaign**.

Links

  • Uses a **branded or owned domain**.
  • Landing page clearly shows the brand.

Opt-Out & Help

  • First message includes **STOP** instructions.
  • **STOP and HELP** responses work properly.

Sending Practices

  • Sends respect local **quiet hours**.
  • Frequency matches what was promised.
  • Traffic isn’t split across multiple numbers.

Registration & Records

  • Brand and campaign are **registered & approved**.
  • Opt-in logs and message samples are stored.
