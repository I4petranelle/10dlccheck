Pre-Send Compliance Checklist

Run this quick checklist before sending to reduce blocks, complaints, and carrier fees.

On this page

    Tip, you can use this page as a lightweight pre-flight. Tick each item as you verify it, then run the message through the checker for automated hints.

    Audience and Consent

    • Contacts explicitly opted in to this campaign or program, records show time, method, and source of consent.
    • Opt-in language is clear about brand, content type, frequency, and fees.
    • No purchased, rented, or shared lists.
    • Opt-in is recent enough for your use case, stale lists are suppressed.

    Identification and Content

    • Brand is named in the first message, for example, “BrandName, your order shipped.”
    • Message is truthful, not deceptive, and not in prohibited categories.
    • Tone and purpose match the registered campaign use case.

    Links and Domains

    • Link uses a branded or first-party domain, not a public shortener.
    • Landing page identifies the brand and matches the message promise.
    • Tracking parameters are clean, avoid suspicious redirects.

    Opt-Out and Help Keywords

    • First message includes STOP instructions when required.
    • STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT are processed immediately.
    • HELP replies return support info and contact details.

    Sending Practices and Cadence

    • Frequency matches what you promised during opt-in.
    • Local quiet hours are respected, sensitive audiences are time-boxed.
    • Traffic is not split across many numbers to evade filtering.
    • Content is personalized and expected for the audience segment.

    Registration and Numbers

    • Brand registration is approved and matches legal entity information.
    • Campaign is approved for this use case, sample messages align with copy.
    • Numbers are properly assigned, verified, and mapped to the campaign.

    List Hygiene and Deliverability

    • Recent bounces and deactivated numbers are removed.
    • High complaint segments are suppressed or throttled.
    • Shortened links are tested for reliability and reputation.

    Legal and Records

    • Opt-in logs, message samples, and program terms are stored for audits.
    • Privacy policy covers messaging program and data retention.
    • Team knows how to service opt-out and support requests quickly.
