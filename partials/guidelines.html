🔍 10DLC Compliance Guidelines

  • Consent basis: Message only opted-in recipients; keep records (CTIA).
  • Branding: Identify your business early in the message.
  • Opt-out: Provide a clear opt-out path when requested (e.g., “Reply STOP”).
  • Content matching: Align content with what users consented to receive.
  • Professional tone: Avoid profanity or pushy sales copy.
  • Anti-abuse: Avoid snowshoeing, spoofing, and number rotation.

🚫 Restricted Content Categories

  • High-risk Financial: Payday loans, crypto, debt collection.
  • Get Rich Quick: Work-from-home schemes, MLM, gambling, sweepstakes.
  • Third-party Services: Services not directly provided by your brand.
  • Controlled Substances: Tobacco, vaping, cannabis/CBD, illegal drugs.
  • SHAFT Content: Sex, Hate, Alcohol, Firearms, Tobacco, Profanity.
  • Scams & Misleading Claims: Phishing, “verify account,” “risk-free,” “expires today.”
