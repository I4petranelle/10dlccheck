Why 10DLC Compliance Matters

10DLC (10-Digit Long Code) is the U.S. standard for A2P messaging. Carriers require registration and compliant messaging to reduce spam and protect consumers.

Non-compliant messages get filtered, delayed, or blocked‚Äîcosting you money and trust. These guides explain rules, pitfalls, and practical steps to keep your messages deliverable.

Quick Compliance Tips

  • Identify your brand in the first message.
  • Include opt-out instructions (STOP) in promotional messages.
  • Avoid public link shorteners; use branded or full URLs.
  • Avoid SHAFT content (Sex, Hate, Alcohol, Firearms, Tobacco).
  • Collect and document consent before sending messages.
  • Honor opt-out requests promptly.
