Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 28, 2025

At 10DLC Checker, we take your privacy seriously. This Chrome Extension was built to help you quickly check text snippets for compliance, without collecting personal information.

What We Collect

The extension does not collect or store your personal data, browsing history, or messages.

When you highlight text and choose to run a check, that text is briefly sent to our server (10dlccheck.com) so we can return a compliance result. Once the result is shown, the text is not stored or saved.

What We Don’t Collect

  • Your name, email address, or other personal details
  • Passwords, account information, or financial data
  • Health information
  • Your browsing history or location

How We Use Data

The only purpose of processing the highlighted text is to generate a compliance check result. We don’t share, sell, or use this data for advertising, analytics or tracking.
